About Me

If you are reading this, then you want to know more about me. Well, I have seen a few things and accomplished a few more.

I have sailed across the world with only a pocket watch and two clean shirts. I have watched all seasons of every TV show on Netflex, Hulu, and Amazon Prime. I have hiked the entire Appalachian Trail, hiked the Continental Divide Trail, and climbed Mount Everest ... twice.

I have read everything from the Victorian era. I can play the piano, the cello, the trumpet, the saxaphone, the drums, and the piccolo. I have lived in the United States, Australia, Austria, Germany, Coumbia, Costa Rica, Iceland, and Japan.

I have competed in the Tour de France four times, Ironman Kona five times, and swam the English Channel both ways. I've toured with the Rolling Stones, hung out with the Aerosmith, played side by side with Eric Clapton, and sang with Celine Dion.

The one thing I have not accomplished yet is completing The Boot Camp at UNC.
